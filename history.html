Spec
specs/steel-intensity-model/
Summary
Additive increment on NOX-003: recover steel activity by modelling the emission-intensity decline explicitly instead of differencing blindly. Fit a smoothness-controlled secular trend s(t) to the meteo-normalised footprint signal; residual = activity proxy (Li 2024 decomposition). Smoothness chosen by blocked time-series CV on the NO₂ series alone — benchmark never consulted (anti-p-hacking). A smoothness-sensitivity sweep + the s(t) diagnostic are emitted; the report states both signs (levels − decoupling, residual + activity).
Finding
Honest near-null persists; decoupling confirmed. On the partial cube: levels r=−0.35 (p<0.001); explicit-model residual monthly r≈0.14 (peak ≈0.19), comparable to yoy (0.19), below the 0.5–0.75 bar. CV picks a near-linear trend (df=2); the sweep decays monotonically with df (no high-df plateau) — signal real-but-weak, no df choice manufactures it. The gain is methodological honesty (one fewer researcher degree of freedom), not a higher number.
Decisions
Explicit emission-intensity model (CV-selected trend) as the activity-recovery method of record; blind differencing kept only for replicability — recorded in decisions/architecture-decisions.md.
