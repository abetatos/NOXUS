Resolved questions

R1Is the sweep feasible from the existing cube (no re-fetch)?Resolved 2026-06-14
Decision
Yes. The committed no2_cube_w.nc is 30×51 cells over the wide AOI (lon 117.74–119.32, lat 38.71–40.44) at ~0.035°×0.055°. The tight AOI (0.10°) is a strict subset (clip), and 0.1°/0.25° are block-means of native cells (coarsen). Both axes are pure re-aggregations — no acquisition needed.

Deferred questions

Finer-than-native / per-plant resolution → needs raw-L2 oversampling (flux divergence, NOX-005), explicitly refused here (ERR-002). Diurnal/weekly source separation → GEMS (NOX-007). A formal cross-prefecture panel (true Parubets design) → out of scope; we run the single-AOI analogue (A1).
