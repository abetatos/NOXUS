A1Signal is cluster/sub-cluster level, not per-factoryPending
Assumption
At TROPOMI's ~5.5×3.5 km footprint a steel plant is sub-pixel, and 14 of 26 operating plants have a neighbour within one pixel (median spacing 3.5 km), so NO₂ resolves clusters/sub-clusters, not individual plants.
Reason
Computed from the facilities CSV (nearest-neighbour distances) on 2026-06-13.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
Sets expectations for attribution (NOX-003); doesn't change acquisition. Overstating resolution would mislead later claims.
