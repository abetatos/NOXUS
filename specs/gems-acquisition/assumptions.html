A1No server-side AOI subset; clip client-sideVerified
Assumption
The NIER-ESC channels (Open-API, SFTP, web) deliver full granules; there is no bbox/AOI request, so we download full granules and clip the Tangshan AOI client-side.
Reason
NIER-ESC Open-API docs (getFileDateList/getFileList/getFileItem) expose no spatial-subset parameter; web download is "up to 20 GB / 40 files". Verified via web research 2026-06-14.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If a subset endpoint exists, the download burden collapses and the gate (A2) is moot — a pleasant surprise to confirm at T1.

A2Full-mission download volume is feasible on a server-class machinePending — gated by T1
Assumption
Downloading full granules (~8 scans/day × ~4.7 yr ≈ ~13k granules), clipped-then-deleted, is feasible on a server-style machine within a configured budget. Per-granule size is unmeasured (estimated order 10²–10³ MB ⇒ ~1–4 TB transient).
Reason
Per-granule byte size is not publicly documented; only the field-of-regard extent and pixel size are known. This is the central feasibility unknown and the reason the task is blocked for later server execution.
Risk level
High
Impact if wrong
If the measured volume exceeds budget → no-go (ERR-004); the loop is not built. Mitigations: scoped date windows, clip-then-delete, resumable batching.
Blocks implementation
Yes — resolved by the T1 spike (REQ-000) before T2+.

A3Diurnal sampling can lift steel's variance share (not levels)Pending
Assumption
~8 daytime scans/day resolve the day-of-week cycle, letting source separation (flat steel baseload vs cyclical traffic) raise steel's variance share — the lever NOX-006 proved invisible to TROPOMI's single ~13:30 pass (weekly term flat, 0.5% variance).
Reason
NOX-006 follow-up note explicitly points to a multi-overpass/geostationary sensor; GEMS is that sensor.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If Tangshan's weekly traffic cycle is too weak to separate, the gain does not materialise → honest-null (valid outcome). Does not change the ~40% mean-share ceiling on a levels estimator regardless.

A4Tangshan resolves usably despite off-nadir geometryPending
Assumption
Tangshan (39.6°N, 118.4°E), ~9.6° west of the 128°E sub-satellite point and near the 45°N north edge of the field of regard, is imaged at ~10 km effective footprint — coarser than the 3.5×8 km nominal but still cluster-usable (≈ NOX-002b's ~5 km adequacy decision).
Reason
Nominal 3.5×8 km is near nadir (Korea); the L3 product is documented at ~5 km over Korea and ~10 km elsewhere (NIER NO₂ page), and off-nadir + high latitude degrade it further at Tangshan. GEMS' gain is diurnal frequency, not spatial resolution. Effective footprint to be confirmed from the granule geolocation at T1.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If the pixel is much coarser than expected, the cluster signal blurs into BTH background — still cluster-level, never per-plant.

No

A7A stable-weather autumn window is the right test for a weekly componentPending
Assumption
A few months of September–October (primary 2023; optional 2024 replication) under near-constant meteorology are sufficient and ideal to detect whether a weekly NO₂ component exists, with Chinese public holidays (esp. Golden Week 1–7 Oct) masked from the weekday profile.
Reason
Early autumn in the North China Plain is post-monsoon + pre-heating-season: most stable/persistent weather (low synoptic noise → weekly cycle not swamped), no residential-heating NO₂ confounder (steel+traffic dominate → cleaner separation), low cloud (balanced GEMS coverage across weekdays and ~8 daily scans), good 39.6°N sun angle. Same-season two-year replication holds climate constant while adding robustness. 2023 overlaps the strongest TROPOMI-era regime. See Q5.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If a few autumn months are too short for a stable weekday profile, extend to the same window in more years (still climate-controlled) before widening seasons. If the weekly cycle is genuinely absent (steel baseload dominates, weak traffic cycle) → honest-null (valid outcome).
Blocks implementation
No (sets the default scope; full mission stays opt-in)
