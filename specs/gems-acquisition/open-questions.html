Q5Temporal scope: full mission vs scoped windowResolved
Question
Acquire the full ~Oct-2021→present record, or a scoped window first to bound the download while testing the weekly/diurnal thesis?
Why it matters
Trades statistical power against download volume (Q1); no server-side subset ⇒ narrowing the date range is the only lever to cut download volume.
Decision (2026-06-14, developer)
Scoped, not full mission. Default = a climatologically-stable autumn window, September–October, primary year 2023 (overlaps the strongest TROPOMI-era regime), with optional replication on Sep–Oct 2024 (same season, different year → climate held constant, robustness added). Rationale: post-monsoon + pre-heating-season = near-constant meteorology (weekly cycle not swamped) and no residential-heating NO₂ confounder (cleaner steel↔traffic separation); low cloud ⇒ balanced GEMS coverage across weekdays and the ~8 daily scans; good 39.6°N sun angle. Mask Chinese public holidays — esp. Golden Week (1–7 Oct) — when estimating the weekday profile (they break the 7-day cycle; a holiday flag is recorded per scan, REQ-009). Bounds download to ~50–150 GB/window. Full mission stays opt-in.
