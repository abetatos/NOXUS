A1 Analysis frequency is weekly Pending
Assumption
The common analysis frequency is weekly, matching the CREA Tangshan operating-rate cadence; NO₂ is aggregated up to weekly.
Reason
Benchmark is weekly; weekly maximizes overlapping observations given the short TROPOMI record (2018→).
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
Monthly would more than halve sample size and weaken power; sub-weekly is impossible for the benchmark.

A3 0.00 means "not reported", not a real zero Pending
Assumption
In the CREA CSV, 0.00 in the Tangshan operating-rate column is a placeholder for dates where the weekly series does not report (the file is the union of daily and weekly cadences) and must be treated as NA.
Reason
A 0% blast-furnace operating rate for the whole cluster is physically implausible; inspection on 2026-06-13 showed interleaved 0.00 rows on non-reporting dates.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If a genuine 0 ever occurred it would be dropped; acceptable given implausibility. Treating placeholders as real zeros would badly bias the series.
Blocks implementation
No

A4 CREA CSV snapshot is the source-of-record Pending
Assumption
We treat a dated, committed snapshot of the CREA CSV as the reproducible source-of-record, rather than scraping CREA HTML or licensing a commercial API.
Reason
The CSV is public, clean, contains the exact series, and is far more stable than HTML scraping; commercial APIs violate the public/free constraint.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If the sheet disappears, future refreshes break — but committed snapshots preserve reproducibility of past results.
