Assumptions

A1Native resolution is adequate (compositing, not oversampling)Pending
Assumption
The openEO product is already on a regular ~0.05°×0.035° (~5 km) grid, so this task composites in time rather than oversampling swath footprints. ~5 km native is adequate for a cluster-level signal (decision A, 2026-06-13). Fine ~1 km oversampling needs raw L2 and is deferred (NOX-003).
Reason
Confirmed from the NOX-002a downloads (30×51 grid, ~0.0546°×0.0347°).
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If per-source resolution were required, this product would be insufficient → pivot to the L2 path. But that is out of the project's cluster-level scope.

No

A2Interim AOI mean is a useful, clearly-labelled stand-inPending
Assumption
A naive AOI spatial-mean weekly NO₂ series is worth emitting now to smoke-test the validation engine end-to-end, provided it is unmistakably labelled as pre-attribution (not the NOX-003 predictor).
Reason
Unblocks a first real NO₂→benchmark test cheaply; the mean is a valid (if crude) cluster proxy.
Risk level
Medium
Impact if wrong
If mislabelled it could be mistaken for attribution; mitigated by explicit labelling + provenance.
