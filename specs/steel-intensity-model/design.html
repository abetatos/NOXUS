Technical summary

This is an additive increment on the NOX-003 pipeline, not a rewrite. It inserts a new detrending method â€” an explicit emission-intensity decomposition â€” into the existing noxus/signal/index.py deseasonalize() dispatch, and extends the validation report + analysis battery to surface the decoupling. The identifying model (Li 2024) is:

Meteorology is already removed upstream (NOX-003 regress_out_meteo). The new step fits a smoothness-controlled secular trend s(t) (penalised regression spline by default; LOESS available) on the meteo-normalised series and returns the residual as the activity proxy. The smoothness (effective degrees of freedom) is chosen by blocked time-series cross-validation on the NOâ‚‚ series alone â€” the benchmark is never consulted during selection (NFR-102). The trend s(t) is itself emitted as a diagnostic: it is the decoupling story.

The central identifying assumption is a timescale separation: the retrofit/intensity decline is slow and smooth, while activity varies on shorter timescales. If that separation fails, the method cannot disentangle them â€” which is exactly why a smoothness-sensitivity sweep (REQ-103) is mandatory output rather than a single point estimate, and why the planted-null test (AC-103) must stay flat across all smoothing choices.

The second half of the task is operational: the deferred full-series re-run on the completed cube, with footprint-radius and coverage-threshold tuning, resolving NOX-003 Q1/Q5 and NOX-002b Q2 on real data.

Risks and trade-offs

  • Timescale-separation failure (central risk). If retrofit decline and activity share timescales, no smoothing cleanly separates them (double-diff already showed this). Mitigation: mandatory df-sensitivity sweep (REQ-103), planted-null flatness test (AC-103), honest reporting of an inseparable / null outcome (NFR-102).
  • Researcher degrees of freedom on smoothness. The df knob could be tuned to maximise r. Mitigation: smoothness chosen by CV on the NOâ‚‚ series alone, benchmark never consulted; full config + sweep recorded and audited (REQ-102, NFR-102). This is the deep-review focus item.
  • Step-change retrofits. A smooth trend mis-fits discrete plant upgrades (EDGE-103). Mitigation: residual diagnostics; change-point modelling flagged as future, not this scope.
  • Short sample until the full fetch lands. Methodology is developed/tested on synthetic + partial data; the full-series number is the acceptance gate (AC-106) and may land as a recorded follow-up if the fetch is still running. Rejected: presenting a partial run as a full-series result (ERR-103).
  • Over-engineering. A full Li-2024 econometric decomposition could balloon. Mitigation: scope is one trend term + CV + sweep + dual-sign reporting â€” additive and bounded; OOS/DM engine stays deferred.
  • Paradigm risk (unchanged). Even with explicit intensity modelling the relative-index v1 may stay null; that remains the gate for the go/no-go on flux divergence (NOX-005). Rejected here: flux divergence / raw-L2.
