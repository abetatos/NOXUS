Real (partial-cube) result

On the current partial cube (389 wk / 90 mo, 5 km footprint): the explicit model confirms the decoupling (levels r = −0.35, p<0.001) and recovers a weak activity residual (monthly r ≈ 0.14, peak ≈ 0.19), comparable to yoy (0.19) but without the arbitrary difference-filter choice. CV selects a near-linear trend (df = 2); the sweep decays monotonically with df (no high-df plateau) — the signal is real-but-weak, below the 0.5–0.75 bar. Honest near-null persists; the methodological gain is removing a researcher degree of freedom and reporting the decoupling as a finding. The full-series re-run remains the go/no-go for NOX-005.
