Task IDNOX-004
Feature slugsteel-event-catalyst
Statusspec_draft

Overview

All statistics verified on synthetic planted fixtures, never live data. The headline guarantees: the detector recovers planted events and stays quiet on noise; weather/cloud spikes are not events; the market study recovers a planted CAR and is null when returns are unrelated; and a no-look-ahead guard proves no future information enters the signal. No network in tests.
